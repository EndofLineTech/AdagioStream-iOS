# Privacy Policy

**Adagio Stream**
Last updated: April 15, 2026

## Overview

Adagio Stream is an IPTV audio streaming app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

**Adagio Stream does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us.** The app does not include analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.

## Data Stored on Your Device

All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us. We have no access to it.

- **Provider credentials** — Server URLs, usernames, and passwords you enter for Xtream Codes or M3U providers are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device.
- **Favorites and playlists** — Your favorited channels, saved songs, custom playlists, and favorite groups are stored locally.
- **Preferences** — App settings including appearance, sort order, buffer duration, text size, and last played channel.
- **Cached images** — Channel logos and artwork are cached on your device to improve performance.
- **Debug logs** — When enabled by you in Settings, the app writes diagnostic logs to your device. These logs automatically redact credentials (passwords and usernames are masked). Logs may contain channel names, server hostnames, and connection timing. Debug logging is off by default and logs are never transmitted unless you explicitly share them.

## External Services

The app connects to the following external services during normal use:

### Services You Configure

- **Streaming providers** — The app connects to streaming servers that you configure (M3U URLs, Xtream Codes providers). Your credentials are sent directly to these servers to authenticate and retrieve channel data. We do not operate, control, or have access to these servers. Your use of these services is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

### Third-Party APIs

The app queries the following public APIs to enhance your experience. No personal data, device identifiers, or account credentials are sent in these requests.

- **ESPN.com API** — Used to retrieve live sports scores for matched sports channels. Only public scoreboard endpoints are queried. No user data is sent.
- **xmplaylist.com API** — Used to retrieve track metadata (song titles, artist names, artwork) for SiriusXM channels. Only channel identifiers are sent. No user data is included.

All third-party API requests include only a generic app user-agent header (`AdagioStream/1.0`). No cookies, device identifiers, or personal information are transmitted.

## Tracking and Analytics

- No analytics or telemetry SDKs are included.
- No advertising SDKs are included.
- No device identifiers (IDFA, fingerprinting, etc.) are collected or transmitted.
- The app's privacy manifest declares `NSPrivacyTracking = false` with no tracking domains and no collected data types.

## Data Sharing

We do not collect, sell, share, or transfer any personal data to third parties.

## Your Data Rights

Under GDPR and similar privacy regulations, you have the following rights:

- **Right to access** — All your data is stored locally on your device. You can view your accounts, favorites, playlists, and settings directly in the app.
- **Right to data portability** — You can export all your data from Settings.
- **Right to erasure** — You can delete all app data from Settings, or by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data including Keychain entries.
- **Right to rectification** — You can edit or update your provider accounts, favorites, and preferences at any time within the app.

## Data Retention

All data is stored on your device only. There is no cloud sync, no server-side storage, and no backups of sensitive data. Data persists until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app.

## Children's Privacy

Adagio Stream does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
